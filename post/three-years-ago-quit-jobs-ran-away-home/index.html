Three Years Ago We Quit Our Jobs And Ran Away From Home

Tue Mar 25, 2014 |
  • News
|

Today. Three years ago today was our last day at work. We celebrated with friends and co-workers, we said our goodbyes.

Within a week we had finished cleaning out our apartment and were officially homeless.

A lot has happened since then.

We lived in a pop-up camper for several months


We saw amazing things all over western America

We visited family and friends


We moved to Mexico


We saw amazing things all over Mexico and Central America

Most importantly, we broke out of the cycle. We did the unexpected, the unusual. We changed the course of our lives.  Instead of doing what everyone else does and following our expected path, we took the next exit.

We Escaped Normal.

For three years now we have been traveling, seeing new places and meeting new people. We have been collecting memories and experiences. And we’ve been sharing them with you, our friends and family and followers. Thanks for coming along for the ride!

And now, on to the next three…
